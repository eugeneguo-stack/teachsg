# Binomial Expansions
[stextbox caption="Binomial Expansions" stextbox id="default"]$(a+b)^n = a^n + \dbinom{n}{1}a^{n-1} b + \dbinom{n}{2}a^{n-2} b^2 + ... $
$+ \dbinom{n}{r}a^{n-r} b^r + ... +  b^{n}$[/stextbox]

[stextbox caption="General Term" stextbox id="download"]$T_{r+1}=\dbinom{n}{r}a^{n-r} b^r$[/stextbox]

[stextbox caption="$n$ choose $r$" stextbox id="info"]$\dbinom{n}{r}=\dfrac{n!}{r!(n-r)!} = \dfrac{n(n-1)...(n-r+1)}{r!}$[/stextbox]
