# Quadratic Equations & Inequalities
[stextbox caption="Sum & Product Of Roots" stextbox id="default"]Sum of roots: $\alpha+\beta=-\dfrac{b}{a}$
Product of roots: $\alpha\beta=\dfrac{b}{a}$[/stextbox]

[stextbox caption="Quadratic Equation From Roots" stextbox id="download"]$x^2-\text{ (sum of roots) }x+\text{ (product of roots) }=0$[/stextbox]

[stextbox caption="2, 1 or 0 real roots" stextbox id="info"]2 real roots: $b^2-4ac>0$
1 real root (2 equal roots): $b^2-4ac=0$
0 real roots: $b^2-4ac<0$[/stextbox]

[stextbox caption="Curve Always Positive / Negative" stextbox id="default"]$b^2-4ac<0$ (because curve has 0 real roots)[/stextbox]

[stextbox caption="Line & Curve" stextbox id="download"]Line intersect curve (at 2 points): $b^2-4ac>0$
Line tangent to curve: $b^2-4ac=0$
Line does not intersect curve: $b^2-4ac<0$
*Line meets curve: $b^2-4ac\geq0$
[/stextbox]
