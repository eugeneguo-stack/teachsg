<strong>Matrix</strong>
Matrix: rectangular array of numbers
Dimension of matrix: number of rows $\times$ number of columns
$A_{ij}$: $i$, $j$ entry in the $i^{th}$ row, $j^{th}$ column

e.g. $$\begin{bmatrix}
  a & b & c \newline 
  d & e & f \newline 
  g & h & i \newline 
  j & k & l
 \end{bmatrix}$$
dimension: $4\times3$ or $\mathbb{R^{4\times3}}$
$A_{11}=a$
$A_{32}=h$

<strong>Vector</strong>
Vector: $n\times1$ matrix
$v_{i}$: $i^{th}$ element

e.g. $$\begin{bmatrix}
  a  \newline 
  b \newline 
  c 
 \end{bmatrix}$$
dimension: 3-dimensional vector or $\mathbb{R^{3}}$
$v_{1}=a$
$v_{3}=c$

1-indexed vector: $$\begin{bmatrix}
  y_1  \newline 
  y_2 \newline 
  y_3 
 \end{bmatrix}$$

0-indexed vector: $$\begin{bmatrix}
  y_0  \newline 
  y_1 \newline 
  y_2 
 \end{bmatrix}$$
