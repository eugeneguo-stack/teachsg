# Proofs In Plane Geometry
[stextbox caption="Properties Of Circles" stextbox id="default"]1. tan. $\perp$ rad.
2. rt. $\angle$ in semicircle
3. $\angle$s in same seg.
4. $\angle$ at centre = 2 $\angle$ at circ.
5. $\angle$s in opp. seg. ($a+c=b+d=180^\circ$)
6. tan. from ext. pt.
[/stextbox]

\end{array}$$[/stextbox]

[stextbox caption="Midpoint Theorem" stextbox id="info"]If $D$ and $E$ are the midpoints of $AB$ and $AC$ respectively, then $DE$ // $BC$ and $DE=\dfrac{1}{2}BC$.[/stextbox]

[stextbox caption="Tangent-Chord Theorem (Alternate Segment Theorem)" stextbox id="default"]If $DE$ is a tangent to the circle at $B$, then $\angle CAB=\angle CBE$ and $\angle ACB=\angle ABD$.
[/stextbox]
