# Vectors In 2 Dimensions
[stextbox caption="Representation" stextbox id="default"]Vectors can be represented by $\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}$ or $\textbf{a}$.
[/stextbox]

[stextbox caption="Magnitude" stextbox id="download"]$|\textbf{a}| = \sqrt{x^2+y^2}$
[/stextbox]
